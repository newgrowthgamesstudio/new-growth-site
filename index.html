The Game

Project: HIJACK delivers a dynamic vehicular heist experience on a resource-strapped frontier world.

Players embody operatives of the Badlands Alliance, scavenging corporate remnants amid minimal oversight. The gameplay loop orchestrates emotional arcs: meticulous preparation yields to visceral combat and vehicular pursuits.


Roguelike progression via garage-based customization and mission selection drives long-term engagement, with player agency shaping the morally complex narrative of the Alliance.

Before You Apply

We operate on a transparent Revenue Share model with a focus on professional collaboration.

What We Offer

  • Revenue Share: Earn a direct share of the project's long-term revenue upon release.
  • Remote & Flexible: 100% Asynchronous remote work. Contract from anywhere in the USA.
  • Portfolio Growth: Build a commercial title in UE5 to expand your professional portfolio.

Requirements

  • US Authorization: Must be permanently authorized to work in the USA (No sponsorship).
  • B2B Compliance: Must operate as an independent contractor/B2B entity.
  • Equipment: You must provide your own professional software and hardware.
